MUST WATCH Kdramas of ALL TIME!!!
9 April, 2022

  1. Goblin The Lonely And Great God


    A CLASSIC!!! Power packed with astounding twists, heartbreaks, mystery and ofcourse heartwarming romance, Goblin is a MUST WATCH!

  2. Strong Woman Do Bong Soon


    Cute Hyung-Sik (Male Lead), even cuter Ji Soo (Second Lead) and more importantly, the humour potrayed by adorable Park Bo Young... Its totally binge worthy! Hey, they got Kim Won-hae! What else do you want? WARNING: Second Lead Syndrome is REAL and SEVERE in this drama

  3. Welcome to Waikiki


    I swear this drama helped me survive the lockdown!!! It's THE MOST FUNNIEST Kdrama EVER!!! THE MOST! PERIOD.

  4. Mr Queen


    Shin Hae Sun at her Best! One of the most funniest and witty Kdramas I have ever seen. And DAMN the romance between those two! Available on Amazon Prime!!

  5. It's ok to not be ok


    Need of the hour. A Kdrama where both the leads suffer from mental trauma and help each other heal it. Seo Ye-ji and Kim Soo-hyun captivate you to your screens with their tremendous performance! Available on Netflix!
